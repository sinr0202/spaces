Our Story

Welcome to Spaces Niagara! We believe life is simply too short to not spend it doing what you love, and we're passionate about helping you achieve that. Founded in the summer of 2017 in a tiny room on Queen street, we're a growing network of founder-friendly coworking spaces in downtown Niagara for hackers, hustlers and hardworking creators of all kinds. We provide vibrant workspaces, an active community and an array of services to help our members succeed. Give your hustle a home.
